Madhya Pradesh - The Heart of Incredible India

A Confluence of Cultures

Madhya Pradesh, aptly named the "Heart of India," is a rich tapestry of diverse cultures and historical influences. Discover ancient temples echoing with spirituality, significant archaeological sites like the Sanchi Stupa and the prehistoric rock shelters of Bhimbetka. Experience the vibrant traditions of tribal communities with their unique art forms and captivating dances.

A Culinary Melting Pot

The cuisine of Madhya Pradesh offers a delightful variety, influenced by different regions and historical periods. Start your day with the popular Poha (especially in Indore), savor the hearty Dal Bafla, and enjoy the unique Bhutte ki Kees. For meat lovers, the aromatic Rogan Josh is a treat. End your meal with the sweet indulgence of Jalebi.

Enchanting Escapes

  • Khajuraho: Be mesmerized by the UNESCO World Heritage temples adorned with intricate sculptures that depict various aspects of life and art. These temples are a testament to India's rich artistic heritage.
